What is full Stack Developer?

A full-stack developer is a versatile software engineer who can work on both the front-end and back-end of a web or mobile application. They are proficient in designing and developing the user interface (front-end) as well as the server-side logic and database management (back-end). Essentially, they handle all aspects of a project, from the user-facing elements to the underlying infrastructure.Full-stack developers may be creative, graphically inclined, internet- and tech-savvy, and have excellent attention to detail.
