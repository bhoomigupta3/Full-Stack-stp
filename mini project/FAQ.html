Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Travel

Q: What destinations do you offer tours to?

A: We offer tours to popular domestic and international destinations including historical sites, beaches, hill stations, and cultural landmarks.

Q: Are your tours suitable for families and kids?

A: Yes, we offer family-friendly packages that include kid-friendly activities and accommodations.

Booking

Q: How can I book a tour package?

A: You can book online through our website, by calling our customer service, or visiting our office directly.

Q: Can I customize my travel package?

A: Absolutely! We offer customizable travel packages to suit your preferences, budget, and travel dates.

Q: How far in advance should I book my trip?

A: We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak travel seasons.

Payment

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash payments at our office.

Q: Is there any deposit required to confirm a booking?

A: Yes, a minimum deposit of 25% is required to confirm your booking. The balance must be paid before the trip begins.

General

Q: Will I get a travel guide with my package?

A: Most packages include a professional guide. Check individual package details for confirmation.

Q: What if I face any issue during the trip?

A: Our 24/7 customer support is always ready to assist you. Emergency contact numbers are shared prior to departure.
